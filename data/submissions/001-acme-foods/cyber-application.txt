Cyber Application Summary

Insured: Acme Foods Distribution LLC
Industry: Food distribution and cold storage
Revenue: $28.4M
Employees: 146
Records: Approximately 82,000 customer and vendor records

Systems:
- Microsoft 365
- Cloud-hosted warehouse management system
- EDI ordering platform
- Customer portal for order tracking
- Fleet telematics platform

Controls:
- MFA: Email and VPN enabled; warehouse handheld admin portal pending confirmation
- EDR: CrowdStrike on servers and laptops
- Backups: Daily with quarterly restore tests
- Patch cadence: Critical patches within 15 days
- Security awareness: Annual training and phishing simulations twice per year

Notes:
The insured depends on two third-party logistics and EDI providers. A 24-hour outage would materially affect shipping and customer order processing.
